How I work

  1. Define the goal

    We start with a short discussion about what you want to achieve. Explain a product, generate leads, teach something, or just engage your audience.

  2. Prototype the idea

    I propose 3 simple interactive concepts showing different ways we could achieve the goal. We review them together and choose one direction.

  3. Build the experience

    I design and build the interactive experience based on the chosen concept.

  4. Launch

    You receive the finished project. If needed, I can also help set up analytics and run campaigns to generate leads.

  5. Improve

    After launch, we can refine and improve the experience based on real user data and feedback.

Collaboration

At any stage, your team can join the process.

For example:

  • your designers can create the visual style
  • your team can write or edit the texts
  • your developers can integrate the project into your product or website

This helps keep everything consistent with your brand and workflow.
